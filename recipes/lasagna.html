Lasagna

Description

Nothing beats classic lasagna. And this is the easiest recipe you will ever make.

It can be made ahead and it’s freezer-friendly too!

Ingredients:

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 28-ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • ¼ cup freshly grated Parmesan

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Lightly oil a 9×13 baking dish or coat with nonstick spray.
  2. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Place into oven and bake for 35-45 minutes, or until bubbling.
    Then broil for 2-3 minutes, or until top is browned in spots.
  4. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
