Pizza

Description

Take a little soppressata, some chopped Calabrian chiles, and a drizzle of hot honey, and you'll end up with the pizza of the moment.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ball Artisan Pizza Dough
  • All-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup Basic Pizza Sauce
  • 1 ½ ounces torn fresh mozzarella
  • 1 ounce thinly sliced soppressata
  • ¼ ounce thinly sliced red onion

Steps:

  1. Using floured fingertips, firmly press all over dough, leaving a 1/2-inch border.
  2. If cooking in your home oven, preheat to 500°F with a baking steel or large round cast-iron pizza pan (such as Lodge 15-inch) on middle rack.
  3. Form a C-shape with the outer edge of your hand, and press firmly inside dough border to define a 1/2-inch wide ring around edge of dough.
  4. Let cool 15 minutes. Serve, garnished with parsley, if desired.
  5. If baking in home oven, bake at 500°F until edges of crust have puffed slightly, about 3 minutes. Rotate pan 90 degrees, and increase oven temperature to broil.
